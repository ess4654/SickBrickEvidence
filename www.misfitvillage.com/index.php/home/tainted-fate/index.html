Tainted Fate

Be the horror

Tainted Fate is a horror game for VR devices in which you are the horror. Unwillingly summoned from the depths of the nether onto a remote island serving as a prison facility, you must use your demonic abilities to gruesomely take down everything between you and your path back to the underworld.
Massacre those who wish to orchestrate your destiny. Dismember any living being. Spawn your minions to help you in your quest for blood.
